Sirin & Alkonost

Inspired by Viktor Vasnetsov’s painting of the Slavic mythological creatures Sirin and Alkonost, this fashion collection explores duality through silhouette. The dresses translate the contrasting natures of the two figures into form and volume, using softness and structure to embody mythological symbolism. The project reimagines narrative imagery as contemporary fashion through shape, movement, and proportion.

Presented at 2025 SFTI-CMU International conference & Invited exhibition
