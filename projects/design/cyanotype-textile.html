Textile Experiment

Cyanotype Textile Experiments

Inspired by the blue-and-white ornamental language of Gzhel ceramics, this project explores cyanotype printing as a contemporary textile surface technique. Traditional motifs are reinterpreted as negative images through light exposure, transforming familiar cultural symbols into abstract, blue-toned textile compositions. The experiments focus on materiality, contrast, and the dialogue between craft heritage and modern fashion design.

Term project for Fashion Design course, 2025
