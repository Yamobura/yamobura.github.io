3D design

Leather Trench Coat

A design study developed through contemporary fashion collection research and AI-assisted prototyping. The project began with analysis of current collection trends and visual references, which were used to generate initial design concepts through Midjourney. Selected outcomes were then translated into a digital garment prototype using CLO3D, focusing on silhouette, proportion, and material expression in leather.
