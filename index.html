About Projects

Hi, my name is Lidiia.

I am a master’s student in Fashion Technology with a background in IT field. My work focuses on how AI, particularly large language models and diffusion models, can support fashion design, styling, and creative exploration.

I develop web-based tools and research-driven prototypes that connect technology with design practice, while also maintaining hands-on experience in fashion through textile experiments, digital garment prototyping and conceptual collections.
